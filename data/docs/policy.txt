Privacy Policy:
We collect and store user data such as name, email, shipping address, and payment details solely to improve user experience, process orders, and provide customer service. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties. You may request data deletion at any time by contacting support.

Shipping Policy:
All orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Standard shipping takes 3–7 business days. Express shipping (₹99 extra) delivers within 2–3 days in metro cities. A tracking number will be provided via email once the order is dispatched.

Refund Policy:
Once we receive the returned item and verify its condition, refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days. Refunds are not eligible for items returned in used or damaged condition. Digital products are non-refundable.

Cancellation Policy:
You may cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it or before the order is marked as "shipped". After that, cancellations are not guaranteed. To cancel, contact customer service via phone or email.
